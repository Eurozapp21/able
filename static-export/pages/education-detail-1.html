Pediatric Rehabilitation Training

Specialized training program for therapists working with children with disabilities. Learn evidence-based techniques and develop expertise in pediatric rehabilitation.

Home / Education / Pediatric Rehabilitation Training

Course Overview

This comprehensive 3-day training program is designed for occupational therapists, physiotherapists, and other healthcare professionals working with children. The course covers evidence-based assessment and intervention strategies specifically tailored for pediatric populations.

Who Should Attend

  • Occupational Therapists working with children
  • Physiotherapists specializing in pediatrics
  • Speech and Language Therapists
  • Special Education Teachers
  • Healthcare professionals in pediatric settings

Learning Outcomes

Assessment Skills

Master comprehensive pediatric assessment techniques and standardized tools.

Intervention Strategies

Learn evidence-based intervention approaches for various pediatric conditions.

Family-Centered Care

Develop skills in working with families and caregivers effectively.

Equipment Expertise

Gain knowledge of adaptive equipment and assistive technology for children.

Course Curriculum

Day 1: Foundations of Pediatric Rehabilitation

  • Child development principles
  • Pediatric conditions and disabilities
  • Assessment tools and techniques
  • Family-centered practice models

Day 2: Intervention Strategies

  • Sensory integration therapy
  • Motor skills development
  • Cognitive rehabilitation techniques
  • Adaptive equipment selection and fitting

Day 3: Advanced Techniques & Case Studies

  • Complex case management
  • Technology integration in therapy
  • Outcome measurement and documentation
  • Professional development and continuing education

Course Instructors

Dr. Elena Nicolaou

Lead Instructor & Pediatric Occupational Therapist

Dr. Nicolaou has over 15 years of experience in pediatric rehabilitation and is a certified instructor in sensory integration therapy. She holds a PhD in Occupational Therapy and has published extensively on pediatric intervention strategies.

Maria Constantinou, PT

Pediatric Physiotherapist

Maria specializes in neurological rehabilitation for children and has been working with pediatric populations for over 12 years. She brings practical experience from both clinical and educational settings.
