AbleTools Announces Strategic Partnership with Leading European Rehabilitation Centers

Published July 10, 2025 | By Business Development Team

AbleTools Ltd is proud to announce a groundbreaking strategic partnership with three of Europe's leading rehabilitation centers, marking a significant milestone in our commitment to advancing rehabilitation care across the continent.

This comprehensive partnership will establish AbleTools as the preferred equipment and training provider for the German Rehabilitation Institute in Berlin, the Swiss Center for Neurological Rehabilitation in Zurich, and the Nordic Rehabilitation Foundation in Stockholm.

Expanding Our European Footprint

The partnership represents a major expansion of AbleTools' presence in the European rehabilitation market. Over the next three years, we will be supplying advanced rehabilitation equipment, providing specialized training programs, and offering ongoing technical support to these premier institutions.

"This partnership is a testament to the quality and innovation of our rehabilitation solutions," said Andreas Dimitriou, CEO of AbleTools. "Working with these world-class centers will allow us to reach thousands more patients and healthcare professionals across Europe."

"AbleTools' commitment to innovation and quality makes them the ideal partner for our rehabilitation centers. Their equipment and training programs will significantly enhance our ability to provide world-class care to our patients."

Training and Knowledge Exchange

A key component of this partnership involves the establishment of joint training programs and knowledge exchange initiatives. AbleTools will be conducting quarterly training sessions at each partner center, while also hosting European rehabilitation professionals at our Cyprus headquarters for intensive certification programs.

The partnership will also facilitate research collaboration, with AbleTools working alongside European researchers to develop next-generation rehabilitation technologies and treatment protocols.

Technology Innovation Hub

As part of this agreement, AbleTools will establish a European Technology Innovation Hub in Berlin, which will serve as a center for equipment demonstration, training, and technical support for all European partners and clients.

The hub will feature the latest rehabilitation technologies, including our new HUR pneumatic equipment range, advanced multi-sensory therapy rooms, and cutting-edge assistive technology solutions.

Looking Ahead

This partnership positions AbleTools for continued growth in the European market and reinforces our mission to provide innovative rehabilitation solutions that improve quality of life for individuals with disabilities.

The formal partnership agreement was signed during a ceremony at the European Rehabilitation Conference in Vienna, with representatives from all partner organizations in attendance.

For more information about this partnership and its benefits for rehabilitation professionals, please contact our European Partnership Division at partnerships@abletools.com.cy.
